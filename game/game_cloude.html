Crisis Management

The Financial Blockchain Game · Nim Strategy

Authors: Kostiuchenko Oleg and Claude Sonnet & Anthropic AI

📋 Game Rules

Scenario: A financial firm has 3 accounts in crisis. Two managers take turns moving funds to the blockchain. The manager who cannot make a move (all accounts zero) is fired.

Rules: On your turn choose any non-empty account and transfer a positive amount. The player who transfers the last coins wins, the other is fired!

Strategy (XOR Nim): XOR of all three accounts is your position indicator.
● XOR ≠ 0 — you are in a winning position (can force XOR=0 for opponent).
● XOR = 0 — you are in a losing position against perfect play.
