Crisis Management

The Financial Blockchain Game

Authors: Claude Sonnet & Anthropic AI

📋 Game Legend

Scenario: A financial company with 3 accounts is in crisis. Two managers take turns transferring funds to the Bitcoin blockchain. The manager who cannot move (all accounts are zero) is fired.

Rules: On your turn, pick any account with coins and transfer any non-zero amount. The player who empties the last coins wins — the other is fired!

Strategy: Use the XOR-equilibrium of all three accounts. If XOR = 0, you're in a losing position. If XOR ≠ 0, you can force a win!
